    Refund Policy

    Last Updated: April 27, 2024

    1. Commitment to Quality

    At SignalNet, we are dedicated to providing high-quality trading signals to enhance your trading strategies. We offer a refund policy under specific conditions outlined below. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations.

    2. Eligibility Criteria for Refunds

    2.1. Strict Adherence to Trading Signals

    • Provide verifiable, unaltered screenshots demonstrating full compliance with each trading signal as issued by SignalNet.
    • All trades must be executed exclusively on the MEXC exchange with a minimum capital investment of $1,000 and must not exceed a leverage ratio of 2x.
    • Submit a detailed and timestamped trading log covering a continuous period of 30 days, adhering strictly to our specified trading protocols.
    • Maintain consistent trading activity without significant deviations from the recommended strategies.

    2.2. Comprehensive Verification Process

    Upon receiving your refund request, our dedicated team will undertake an exhaustive review of your trading activities over the 60-day subscription period. This includes:

    • Cross-referencing trade execution times and prices against the provided signals.
    • Verifying adherence to leverage restrictions and capital investment requirements.
    • Assessing the integrity and completeness of the submitted trading logs.

    Any inconsistencies, deviations, or evidence of non-compliance will result in the immediate denial of your refund request.

    2.3. Performance Assessment Parameters

    To qualify for a refund, your account must exhibit a performance indicator of zero or negative returns despite strict compliance with all provided signals. The assessment will consider:

    • Overall profitability during the subscription period.
    • Consistency in following trading signals without unauthorized modifications.
    • Impact of external market factors beyond SignalNet's control.

    If the performance metrics align with your refund claim, a full refund will be processed along with an additional 20 trading signals to enhance your future trading strategies. Should there be discrepancies between your performance and the refund claim, SignalNet reserves the right to offer a partial refund of up to 50% or deny the request entirely based on the evaluation.

    2.4. Mandatory Trading Commitment

    Eligibility for a refund requires active and consistent trading using SignalNet's signals for a minimum of 30 days within the initial 60-day subscription period. Irregular trading behavior, such as sporadic adherence or ignoring multiple signals, will render the refund request ineligible.

    3. Procedure to Request a Refund

    1. Submit a formal refund request by emailing support@signalnet.com or through the designated support portal on our website.
    2. Include the following information in your request:
      • Your registered email address and user ID.
      • Purchase details, including subscription type and date.
      • Comprehensive screenshots of trades executed in alignment with our signals.
      • A detailed trading log covering the required period.
      • A written statement affirming adherence to all SignalNet guidelines.
    3. Ensure all submitted documents and logs are clear, legible, and free from any alterations.

    4. Refund Processing Guidelines

    Upon receiving your refund request, SignalNet will:

    • Conduct a thorough examination of all submitted materials.
    • Engage in direct communication with you if additional information or clarification is required.
    • Notify you of the decision regarding your refund within 10-15 business days.
    • If approved, issue the refund along with 20 additional trading signals to your account within 5-7 business days.

    Please note that processing times may vary based on your financial institution and payment provider.

    5. Ineligible Refund Conditions

    • Refund requests submitted beyond the 60-day post-purchase window.
    • Accounts that have not adhered strictly to trading signals or have utilized leverage exceeding the stipulated 2x ratio.
    • Submission of incomplete, falsified, or tampered evidence.
    • Refund requests arising from market conditions unrelated to SignalNet's service quality.
    • Orders placed through affiliate links or third-party resellers are non-refundable.

    SignalNet reserves the exclusive right to decline any refund request that does not meet the outlined criteria or is suspected to involve fraudulent activity.

    6. Additional Terms and Conditions

    • Any attempts to deceive, manipulate, or provide false information during the refund process will result in immediate disqualification and potential account termination.
    • SignalNet reserves the right to modify, amend, or terminate this Refund Policy at any time without prior notice.
    • All refunds are subject to the final decision of the SignalNet management team.

Any significant changes will be reflected by updating this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about our refund procedures.
